# Quantium Data Analytics Project -- Category and Customer Analytics

## Project Overview

This project replicates a typical customer analytics engagement at Quantium, focusing on purchasing behaviour in the chips category. The objective is to support a Category Manager with data-driven insights for an upcoming category review.

The analysis explores who is buying chips, how they are buying, and what factors drive their purchasing decisions. The end goal is to translate these findings into practical recommendations that can inform category strategy.

---

## Business Objective

To identify key customer segments and understand their purchasing behaviour in order to:

* Improve targeting of high-value customers
* Optimise product assortment and pack sizes
* Support pricing and promotional decisions

---

## Approach

The project follows a structured, end-to-end analytics workflow:

1. Data preparation and quality checks
2. Feature engineering (e.g. pack size, brand extraction)
3. Customer-level metric development
4. Segmentation analysis using LIFESTAGE and PREMIUM_CUSTOMER
5. Exploration of purchasing drivers (frequency, spend, brand, pack size)
6. Translation of insights into business recommendations

---

## Key Questions

* Which customer segments contribute the most to chip sales?
* How do purchasing behaviours differ across segments?
* What factors influence spend (e.g. frequency, pack size, brand preference)?
* Where are the opportunities for growth within the category?

---

## Expected Outputs

* Cleaned and structured dataset ready for analysis
* Customer-level metrics and segment comparisons
* Visualisations of purchasing behaviour
* Clear, commercially relevant recommendations
